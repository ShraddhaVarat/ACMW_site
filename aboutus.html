The ACM India Council council is focused on a wide range of ACM activities including: serving as a professional network for technology of computing, encouraging students to take an active interest in the world of computing, facilitating the organization of ACM conferences in India.

ACMW

ACM-W supports and advocates internationally for the full engagement of women in all aspects of the computing field, providing a wide range of programs and services to ACM members and working in the community to advance the contributions of technical women.
